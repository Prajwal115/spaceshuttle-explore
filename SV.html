Support Vehicles

The Space Shuttle's operations were supported by vehicles and infrastructure that facilated its transportation , constructions and crew access . The Crawler transporters Carried the MLP and the Space Shuttle from the VAB to the launch site . The Shuttle Carrier Aircraft (SCA) were two modified Boeing 747s that could carry an orbiter on its back . The Crew Transport Vehicle (CTV) was modified airport jet bridge that was used to assist Astronauts to egrens from the orbiter after landing . The astrovans transported Astranauts from the crew quaters in the operational and checkout building to the launch pad on launch day .
